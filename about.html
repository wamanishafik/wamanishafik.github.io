About Evergreen Nursery

For over 7 years, we've been helping plant lovers create beautiful, thriving green spaces.

Our Story

Evergreen Nursery began in 2017 with a simple mission: to make quality plants and expert gardening advice accessible to everyone. What started as a passion has grown into Kampala's premier plant nursery, but our core values remain the same.

Our nursery was born from a childhood passion for plants. Today, we're proud to serve both novice plant parents and experienced gardeners with the same dedication and care. Our team of horticulturists, designers, and plant enthusiasts is committed to helping you find the perfect plants for your space and providing the guidance needed to help them thrive.
